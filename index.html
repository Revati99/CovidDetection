About the Project

SARS-CoV-2 has been the worst pandemic in over 100 years.We have put forth this resource which will provide radiology reports and detect Covid-19 virus through patient CT-scan reports.

Sentiment studies for various social media platforms along with current tracker of covid-19 across the world will also be displayed.

Covid Detection

Introduction

The data collection for covid-19 detection is taken from Negin Medical located at Sari in Iran. This dataset is combined using three different data collections.The images are HRCT radiology scans which are in the format of 16-bit DICOM format and converted into TIFF format as grayscale images.The dataset used for sentiment study is taken from twitter.The data contains location, time and date of tweet along with the original tweet in the form of text.The rest of the study comprises of daily incremental data taken from data world website and is used for visualization. It consists of bubble structure for density of covid cases in that country and includes cases count and death count of the region that the user has selected on the map.

Our goal is to provide this evidence based analysis for people who do not have the resources or access to a radiologist immediately.We do not promote using this webpage as the sole source of diagnosis. A professional should be consulted for further analysis and medication.
